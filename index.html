Invention of Money

An insight into the history and invention of money.

This is money.

No one knows for sure who first invented such money, but historians believe metal objects were first used as money as eary as 5,000 B.C. Around 700 B.C., the Lydians became the first Western culture to make coins. Other countries and civilisations soon began to mint their own coins with specific values.
